Resume.

I started my career as a marketing generalist at Visa, single-handedly driving $8M in revenue for a prepaid gaming product. The experience was invaluable and exposed me to almost every facet of marketing.

Realizing the importance of specialization, I decided to pursue lifecycle marketing and spent nearly a decade at Coupons.com, learning and implementing personalization, automation, & channel expansion strategies from the ground up.

Since then, I've taken that zero-to-one muscle across industries — scaling lifecycle programs for brands in e-commerce, health tech, and commerce SaaS. I'm currently shipping on the growth lifecycle team at Shopify, generating $30M+ in gross profit to date for the self-serve funnel.

Beyond the 9 to 5, my true passion lies in social enterprise. I dedicate my time to building nonprofits focused on healthcare accessibility and financial empowerment — because meaningful growth should reach everyone.
